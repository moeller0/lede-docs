====== Hardwaredata ======

Pages in the '':toh:hwdata'' namespace are the source of information for the [[toh:start|Table of Hardware]].
The information contained therein is purely technical facts and numbers.

If you are searching for more detailed information, like install descriptions, pictures, mods, etc., please look at the respective devicepage, which you can find via the [[toh:start|Table of Hardware]].
