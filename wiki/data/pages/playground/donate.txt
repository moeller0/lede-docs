LEDE Project is maintained by a global community of volunteers (individuals and companies) and it incurs some monthly [[:infrastructure#servers|infrastructure]] costs.

If you wish to support LEDE projects, there are [[:start#lede_wants_you|multiple ways to do it]] and one of those ways is to donate towards the monthly [[:infrastructure#servers|infrastructure]] costs.

All money collected are controlled by the project leaders and go towards those specific [[:infrastructure#servers|infrastructure]] costs only. You can donate with PayPal, credit card, bitcoin and bottle caps.

If you wish to donate please follow this link. Please note however that when enough money to cover the costs for a foreseeable future is collected, you will no longer be able to donate.
